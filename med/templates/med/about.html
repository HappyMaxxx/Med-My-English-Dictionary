Welcome to My English Dictionary

Our website is designed for everyone who wants to improve their English language skills and make the learning process more engaging and effective. We offer unique tools and features to help you create and manage your own English word dictionary.

Key Features

Create Your Personal Dictionary

Record new words and their translations to never forget them. Add example sentences for better understanding.

Interactive Tests

Create tests based on your words to check your knowledge. Various test formats, including multiple choice, word input, and more.

Group Words

Organize words by topics or categories for more structured learning. Easily find and study words by groups.

Share Words and Groups

Share your words and groups with friends or colleagues. Access other users' dictionaries to expand your vocabulary.

Why Choose Us?

  • Easy-to-use interface: Our website is easy to use, and you'll quickly master all the features.
  • Flexibility and personalization: Customize your dictionary to your needs and preferences.
  • Community: Join a community of users who are also striving to improve their English language skills.
