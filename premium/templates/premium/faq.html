Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Med – My English Dictionary

What is Med – My English Dictionary?

Med is a web application designed to help you learn English by creating a personal dictionary, organizing words into groups, practicing vocabulary, tracking achievements, and connecting with friends.

What are the differences between Free, Standard, and Pro plans?

The Free plan offers basic features like a dictionary with up to 100 words and 3 word groups. The Standard plan ($4.99/month) includes unlimited words, groups, and advanced practice modes. The Pro plan ($9.99/month) adds offline access, API integrations, exclusive games, and priority support. See our Subscriptions page for details.

Can I try premium features before subscribing?

Yes! We offer a 7-day trial for the Pro plan. Visit the Subscriptions page to start your trial.

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage your subscription in your profile settings. If you need help, contact us at mpatik2006@gmail.com.

What payment methods are supported?

We currently support payments via PayPal and other methods. Check the Subscriptions page for more details or contact us for assistance.

How can I contribute to Med?

You can support Med via Ko-fi or PayPal (mpatik2006@gmail.com). Contributions via code or feedback are also welcome on our GitHub page.

Still have questions? Contact us or visit our Subscriptions page.
